# Workflow — New hire onboarding

> A single guided path through the hub. One source, read in order.

1. **Constitution** — read `constitution.md`. These are the rules nothing may break.
2. **Domains** — read `domains.md`. Learn the functional areas, owners, and status.
3. **Vocabulary** — skim `vocabulary.md`. Learn what product terms map to in code.
4. **Pick a domain** — choose the area you'll work in. Read its entry and linked specs in `docs/specs/`.
5. **Explore the graph** — ask Claude "show me how [domain] works across the repos" to get the code slice grounded in the spec.
6. **First change** — find a small task in your domain; check the relevant spec and constitution before opening a PR.

Ask Claude anything along the way — it answers from this hub.
